###  积分作业_1

#### 188.2

(5)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^2\sqrt{x}}
&=\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^{\frac{5}{2}}} \\\\
&=-2x^{\frac{1}{2}} + C
\end{aligned}
$$

(13)

\end{aligned}
$$

$$

$$

(18)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int sec{x}(sec{x}\tan{x})\mathrm{d}x
&=\int sec^2{x}\mathrm{d}x - \int sec{x} \tan{x}\mathrm{d}x \\\\
&= \tan{x} - sec{x} + C
\end{aligned}
$$

(19)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \cos^2{\frac{x}{2}}\mathrm{d}x
&=\int \frac{1}{2}(1+\cos{x})\mathrm{d}x \\\\
&=\frac{1}{2} \int(1+\cos{x})\mathrm{d}x \\\\
&=\frac{1}{2} (\int \mathrm{d}x + \int \cos{x} \mathrm{d}x) \\\\
&=\frac{1}{2}(x +\sin{x}) + C
\end{aligned}
$$

(21)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \frac{\cos{2x}}{\cos{x}-\sin{x}}\mathrm{d}x
&=\int \frac{(\cos{x} - \sin{x})(\cos{x} + \sin{x})}{\cos{x}-\sin{x}}\mathrm{d}x \\\\
&=\int \cos{x}\mathrm{d}x + \int \sin{x} \mathrm{d}x \\\\
&=\sin{x} - \cos{x} + C
\end{aligned}
$$
